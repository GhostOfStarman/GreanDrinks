About Us

Grean is a passion project by two kids who want to create the next best matcha cafe. In the next two years, we’ll be building our brand, vendoring/appearing at pop-ups, and testing new products. Our end-goal is to open a brick-and-mortar space in 2-3 years, however, we want to learn and grow within these next few years and make some friends as we come along.

We’re building in public and documenting our journey as we build something truly compelling. We’re glad you’re here, thank you for supporting.
